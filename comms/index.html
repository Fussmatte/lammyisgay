Reese’s Commission Rules

Please note that this page cannot tell you whether my commissions are open or full. To check, please see my Twitter profile linked below. Also note that I’m testing the waters with this venture; depending on workload, prices may be subject to change.

Basic drawings are $1 (USD) each. Rules for those are as follows:

  • Drawings are low-resolution and aliased (i.e. MS Paint style).
  • You can request any character(s) you want, though I may reject some on the basis of:
    • complexity (i.e. a character is too hard to draw)
    • number (i.e. too many characters in a request)
    • specific characters or people (no characters from offensive media/no real people)
  • No NSFW requests.
  • Anything else minor you want me to add, ask and I’ll see what I can do.

Slightly-Less-Basic drawings are $3 each.

  • Drawings will be higher resolution, drawn digitally via Krita.
  • Rules about subject matter are the same as above.
  • Anything else you want me to add, ask and I’ll try to help you out with it.

Payment will be handled, anonymously on both ends, through Arty. Arty’s accepted payments are VISA, MasterCard and PayPal.

I’m doing the best I can here. Please don't hassle me for the quality of my work or for charging for it; I never went to art school, I’m doing this for those who want it, and after all I need money to get by.

To make a request, head to my Twitter profile @UmJammerLammyPS and shoot me a DM!
